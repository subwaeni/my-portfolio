Decorate your home with plants

Plants are an easy way to transform your space, whether your style is high drama or subtle minimalism. Grouping iconic plants like Fidel, Alto and Nicolau will inspire a sense of elegance and help create a microclimate. Add moments of interest with tiny plants like Spike or Suri.


Creating different levels with your plants is a great way to draw your eye around a room, or even just a corner. Use plant stands and hanging pots to experiment with height. Try grouping a variety of smaller plants and pots to create the same effect on a miniature level.


Pots with natural textures like clay or concrete will keep things sleek, or mix styles, like our dipped pots and ceramic pots, for a more eclectic vibe.
